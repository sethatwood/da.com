Dean Atwood

Chief Information Officer turned Writer

DEAN ATWOOD was born and raised in Rutland, Vermont. He and his wife raised their four children in Crozet, Virginia and have lived there for the past twenty-one years. After finishing a successful career as a Chief Information Officer, he now devotes his time to writing fiction.

   

The Secret Target

What would Thomas Jefferson think about a Defense Intelligence Agency facility being located a few miles from the University of Virginia?
 
 

FBI Agent Quinton Target suspects his NSA cyber analyst girlfriend may be unfaithful. While investigating her suspected infidelity, he becomes entangled with a secret interagency intelligence operation, which is being led by the DIA in Charlottesville, Virginia.

The Retreat Tower

This thriller will take you inside an insane asylum in Brattleboro, VT, where a few, seemingly normal, patients have been committed. The novel will keep you guessing about who’s sane and who’s not.

When a woman falls from an iconic tower located on the grounds of The Brattleboro Retreat, a patient, claiming to be an undercover detective becomes obsessed with finding the truth about her death.

Agua De Valencia

If you like suspense thrillers set in exotic places, you’ll enjoy this escape to Valencia, Spain, where you’ll follow a debonair former FBI agent as he attempts to save a damsel in distress.

On a typical summer evening in Valencia, a former FBI agent thinks he might get lucky with a beautiful woman, but the evening is cut short as he’s drawn into a kidnapping case.
